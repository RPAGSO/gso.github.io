Student Seminars in Physics and Astronomy (SSPAR)

Seminars given by graduate students to practice public speaking and science communication. Often a research talk, but can also be related to grad student life.

Fall 2023

I'm interested in exploring cosmology questions (i.e. Hubble tension, dark matter) through the tool of gravitational lensing and hydrodynamic cosmological simulations. I've worked with pygravlens, lensmodel, halotools, gala, emcee, and simulations like IllustrisTNG and Romulus.
My advisor is Chuck Keeton, and we focus on lens models and physically-motivated priors to improve measurements of the Hubble constant through strongly lensed quasars by massive elliptical galaxies.
